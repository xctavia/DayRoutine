Your Semester 1 routine

Built around your timetable — discipline is the bridge between goals and achievement

The rule: Every class is followed by a review session. Every study block uses the Pomodoro method — 50 min focused work, 10 min break. No screens after 21:00. Non-negotiable wake time: 05:30.
